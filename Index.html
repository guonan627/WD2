Welcome to ML Strength

About Us

ML Strength are Australia's newest brand in health and fitness, with gyms opening throughout the Brisbane metropolitan area. Our business is aimed at a health conscience adult who wants to move beyond the usual local gym. ML Strength believes fitness comes first and keeping fit and improving your health is not just a fad - it's a way of life. Whether your goal is weight loss, body building, nutrition or diet improvements, having more energy, or just looking and feeling better - we're here for you!
